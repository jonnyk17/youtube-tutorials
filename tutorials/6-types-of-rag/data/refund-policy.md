# ShopMax Return and Refund Policy

## Return Window

All items may be returned within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in their original condition with tags attached and original packaging.

## Opened Items

Shoes that have been tried on indoors may be returned. Shoes that show signs of outdoor wear cannot be returned. We inspect all returns and reserve the right to reject items that show signs of use beyond indoor try-on.

## Refund Process

Once we receive your return, please allow 5-7 business days for inspection and processing. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

- **Full refund**: Items returned in original, unworn condition with tags and packaging.
- **Partial refund (80%)**: Items returned without original packaging but in unworn condition.
- **No refund**: Items that show signs of outdoor wear, damage, or missing tags.

## Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. To get a different size or color, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order.

## Sale Items

Items purchased on sale or with a discount code follow the same return policy. Refunds are issued for the amount actually paid.

## Defective Items

If you receive a defective item, contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will provide a prepaid return label and issue a full refund or replacement at no cost. Please include photos of the defect when contacting support.

## How to Start a Return

1. Log in to your account on shopmax.com
2. Go to "Order History"
3. Select the order and click "Start Return"
4. Print the prepaid return label
5. Ship the item back within 7 days

If you checked out as a guest, contact support@shopmax.com with your order number to initiate a return.
