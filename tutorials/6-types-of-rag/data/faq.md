# ShopMax Frequently Asked Questions

## Shipping

**How long does shipping take?**
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. Free shipping is available on orders over $50.

**Do you ship internationally?**
We currently ship to the US, Canada, and the UK. International orders may take 10-14 business days and are subject to local customs duties.

**Can I track my order?**
Yes. Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package on our website under "Order Status."

## Orders

**Can I cancel my order?**
Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters our fulfillment process and cannot be cancelled. You can return the item once received.

**Can I change my shipping address after ordering?**
Address changes are only possible within 1 hour of placing your order. Contact support immediately if you need to update your address.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

## Products

**Are your products authentic?**
Yes. We are an authorized retailer for all brands we carry. Every product comes with a manufacturer warranty.

**How do I find my shoe size?**
We have a size guide on each product page. We recommend measuring your foot in the evening when feet are slightly larger. If you are between sizes, we recommend going up half a size.

**Do you offer gift wrapping?**
Yes. Gift wrapping is available for $5 per item. You can select this option at checkout.
